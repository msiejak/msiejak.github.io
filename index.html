Learn ADB and Fastboot

This app will teach you how to take control of your device and become an Android power user! With over 20 topics covered, you'll learn how to use adb commands to manage files, change settings, control apps, view device info, and much more from the command line. The app also has a beautiful UI and 8 stunning themes!
